Graphic Design

As the Art Director for FMQB, I am responsible for creating the layout for their Pro.QB magazine, a radio-industry magazine that focuses on the Triple A (Adult Album Alternative) format. Not only do I lay out the articles, but I also design all custom graphics and most of the advertisements for the magazine's clients. I also design all the advertisements for FMQB.com's website, and many of the email eCards that are sent to record labels and thousands of radio stations all throughout the country.

I also do all the graphic design for all web pages, websites, and newsletter templates on my web-design page.

Advertisements

The hardest aspect of designing advertisements is meeting a client's expectations. When asked to present a high volume of content within the small canvas of an online ad, it because very difficult to deliver it in an appealing manner without it looking too congested or bland. Tackling these feats frequently requires animation.

The target audience for FMQB.com is those who are within the radio industry; these therefore advertise details like how many spins a song recieves on radio, how many stations have added it to their playlist, or where the song ranks on various charts.

I have created ads for some of the biggest names in the music industry including Arianna, Carly Rae Jepson, Chris Brown, Christina Auguilera, Demo Lovato, The Dirty Heads, Discolsure, Ellie Goulding, Eminem, Florida Georgia Line, Hot Chelle Rae, Imagine Dragons, Justin Timberlake, Kelly Clarkson, Kendrick Lamar, Ke$ha, Lady Gaga, Lana Del Rey, M83, Maroon 5, Of Monsters and Men, OneRepublic, Papa Roach, The Postal Service, Phillip Phillips, P!nk, Robin Thicke, The 1975, Vampire Weekend, Will.I.Am, and Zedd.
